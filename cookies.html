Cookie Overview

This Cookie Policy explains how DriveSwift uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and provide personalized content.

We use cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website traffic. You can manage your preferences below.

Cookie Preferences

Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality. Cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign effectiveness.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website or use our mobile app. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our services.

Session Cookies

Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser

Persistent Cookies

Stored on your device for a set period or until manually deleted

First-Party Cookies

Set directly by DriveSwift when you visit our website

Third-Party Cookies

Set by external services we use (analytics, advertising, etc.)

2. How We Use Cookies

DriveSwift uses cookies for several purposes to enhance your experience and improve our services:

Essential Functionality

Enable core website features, remember login status, and maintain security

Understand how users interact with our site to improve user experience

Personalization

Remember preferences, customize content, and provide relevant recommendations

Marketing & Advertising

Deliver targeted content and measure advertising effectiveness

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Authentication Cookies

Purpose: Keep you logged in and maintain session security

Duration: Session or 30 days

Examples: session_id, auth_token, csrf_token

Preference Cookies

Purpose: Remember your settings and preferences

Duration: 1 year

Examples: language_pref, theme_setting, cookie_consent

Security Cookies

Purpose: Protect against security threats and fraud

Duration: Session

Examples: security_token, rate_limit, device_fingerprint

Analytics Cookies

Google Analytics

Duration: 2 years

Examples: _ga, _gid, _gat

Provider: Google LLC

Internal Analytics

Duration: 1 year

Examples: ds_analytics, page_views, user_journey

Provider: DriveSwift Ltd

Marketing Cookies

Social Media Cookies

Purpose: Enable social media sharing and track referrals

Duration: Varies (30 days - 2 years)

Examples: fbp, _fbq, li_sugr

Providers: Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter

Advertising Cookies

Purpose: Deliver targeted advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness

Duration: 90 days - 2 years

Examples: doubleclick, ads_prefs, campaign_id

Providers: Google Ads, Microsoft Advertising

4. Mobile App Data Storage

In addition to web cookies, our mobile app uses local storage technologies:

  • Local Storage: Store app preferences, progress data, and offline content
  • Secure Storage: Encrypted storage for sensitive information like authentication tokens
  • Database Storage: Store practice questions, results, and user achievements

Mobile App Storage

Mobile app data is stored locally on your device and synchronized with our secure servers when you're online. This ensures your progress is saved and accessible across devices.

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

6.1 Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can:

  • Accept all cookies for the full website experience
  • Manage preferences to choose which cookie categories to allow
  • Reject optional cookies (essential cookies will still be used)

6.2 Browser Controls

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

Google Chrome

Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data

Mozilla Firefox

Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

Microsoft Edge

Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

6.3 Mobile App Settings

In our mobile app, you can manage data collection preferences:

  • Go to Settings → Privacy → Data Collection
  • Toggle analytics and marketing data collection
  • Manage notification preferences
  • Clear local storage and cache

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable certain cookies, some website features may be affected:

Essential Cookies Disabled
Website may not function properly, login issues may occur
Analytics Cookies Disabled
Limited ability to improve user experience based on usage data
Marketing Cookies Disabled
Less relevant advertisements, but core functionality remains

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in:

  • Technologies we use
  • Legal requirements
  • Third-party services
  • User feedback and preferences

We'll notify you of significant changes through our website banner or app notifications.
